ROS 2 + Gazebo Harmonic Casualty Manipulation Simulation Stack is a full simulation environment for collaborative robotics research on ROS 2 Jazzy and Gazebo Harmonic.

It integrates:

- Clearpath Husky mobile base,
- Universal Robots arms and custom end-effectors, and
- a human (caveman) model for interaction studies.

The stack supports rapid setup of multi-agent simulation scenarios for perception, teleoperation, and policy evaluation.
